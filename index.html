Fish Tocino

5 kg fish (Milkfish, Carp, Tilapia etc.)
3 cup soy sauce
1 tbsp ground pepper
3 cloves crushed garlic
3 cup brown sugar
Cooking oil


How They Process:

Step 1:
Fillet the fish. Cut the fillet with 4â€³ length. The cuttings could be used for burger.

Step 2:
Mix the garlic, brown sugar, soy sauce and pepper together to dissolve.

Step 3:
Pour the mixture over the fish fillet.

Step 4:
Soak for 6 hours. Drain

Step 5:
Sun or air-dry until dry to touch. Pack in polyethylene bag and seal.

Step 6:
Keep refrigerated for longer storage.
